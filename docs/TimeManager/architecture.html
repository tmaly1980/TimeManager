Architecture

Projects

Projects organize goals and tasks towards making major changes to a company's products and services, whether internal or external. Projects need not have to do with a particular product.

Milestones

Milestones group the tasks in a project into a well-defined goal. Many tasks belong to a milestone, and many milestones belong to a project.

Task Groups

Task Groups provide an easy way to group related tasks together, to provide a snapshot of the common goal that the tasks work towards.

Tasks

Tasks can belong to any number of milestones, task groups, and parent tasks. They need not belong to task groups, or milestones, and be used purely for task management without any involvement with projects.

Task Dependencies

Task dependencies are special tasks that are required to be done prior or during the completion of another task. The depending task can belong to multiple dependent tasks.

Subtasks

A subtask is a special kind of task whose purpose is to complete a portion of another task, called it's parent task. Subtasks can belong to any number of parent tasks.

Task Specialization

Workers can be assigned certain categories of tasks that they can perform. In other words, they can be designated what types of tasks they are qualified (or preferred by their manager) to do. Multiple people in a group may be qualified to do the same thing.

Task Assignment

Tasks are typically assigned by group managers, but may be assigned by other requestors, if they are designated as an explicit assignment manager for the particular task owner, or are designated as a Site Administrator. Unless you are a site administrator, group manager, or have explicit assignment rights over a particular person, you are not able to assign tasks to others.
