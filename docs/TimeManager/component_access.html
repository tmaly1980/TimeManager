Description:

The Security module provides a way to enforce people only seeing and changing the tasks, milestones and projects that they should. Access is subdivided differently among task requestors, task owners, and managers (who aren't a task's requestor). All other access is not possible (such as looking at someone's task queue who is not in any way hierarchically related).

Goals:

The Security module helps you:

  • Enforce allowing people to view and edit only the projects and tasks that they're supposed to
  • Enforce task assignment in a hierarchical structure
  • Enforce the proper requirements and specifications of a task to be defined only by the task's requestor
  • Enable managers other than the task's requestor to see your tasks, but not alter them
Features:

The Security module consists of the following major parts:

  • Task Requestor Access: Permit the requestor of a task (and no one else) to create, reassign and alter the specifications of a task
  • Task Owner Access: Permit the task owner to alter the status of a task, without permitting changes to the task's requirements and specifications
  • Non-Requestor Managerial Access: Permit managers of a task owner, other than the task's requestor, to view, but not alter their tasks
  • Other Access: All other people will not be able to find, look at or alter tasks and projects of others that they have no managerial access over
