Description:

The dashboard is the main menu provided when first logging into the system. It provides a list of common things to do including adding tasks and projects, viewing the documentation and searching for tasks. It also displays tasks and projects you and your team have due in the next two weeks. For your convenience, overdue items show in red, and completed items show in blue.

Goals:

The Dashboard module helps you:

  • Monitor tasks and projects that you and your team have due
Features:

The Dashboard module consists of the following major parts:

  • Common Actions: Jump to do common actions such as adding and searching tasks and projects, as well as browsing the documentation
  • My Tasks/Projects: Show the tasks and projects that you have due this and next week
  • My Workers' Tasks/Projects: Keep track of tasks and projects that your workers have due this and next week
Overview:

Here's what the module's major parts look like:

Dashboard Common Actions:
Quick links to doing common things, such as adding tasks, projects, searching, and viewing documentation.
My Tasks:
View your tasks due both this and next week. Completed items are in blue, wherease overdue items are in red.
My Workers' Tasks:
View the tasks that your workers have due this and next week. If they are already completed, they will show up in blue.
My Projects:
View your projects that are due this and next week. Completed projects will show up in blue, whereas overdue projects show up in red.
My Workers' Projects:
View the projects that your workers have due this and next week. Completed projects show up blue, whereas overdue projects show up red.
Getting There:
Dashboard Console:
By clicking on the 'Dashboard' tab in the main menu, you can reach the dashboard.
System Login:
By logging into the system, you will be automatically taken to the dashboard.
