Description:

The Task Management module provides a means to manage your time and the time of your team spent on tasks. It helps you organize, distribute, and resolve tasks quickly. Sections within a task being viewed/edited can be hidden or shown by clicking on the check box next to the section's header.

Goals:

The Task Management module helps you:

  • Find tasks that you have time available for
  • Assign, manage, and monitor tasks and projects to your team members
  • View and redistribute your team's workload via graphs
  • Manage subtasks and dependencies
  • Perform changes to multiple tasks simultaneously
  • Keep track and manage a rapidly dynamic queue of tasks
  • Eliminate redundant work by referencing existing tasks
  • Keep track of how much time is spent on certain types of tasks, to justify hiring dedicated workers or increased budgets
  • Find tasks based upon specifications, timeline, status, participants or text
Features:

The Task Management module consists of the following major parts:

  • Show/Hide (Power/Basic User modes): Showing or hiding sections of a task for the complexity or simplicity that you want.
  • Task Specifications: Give a task a brief summary, an extensive description, priority and what product (if any) it belongs to.
  • Timeline: Keep track of when and in how much time you want a task done, as well as get estimates from the task's owner.
  • Progress: Manage and monitor the progress of a task, including it's status, cost and what stage it's in (relative to what kind of task it is). Store information in an arbitrary amount of notes.
  • Categorization: Organize tasks by it's purpose, and automatically assign it to someone qualified.
  • Products: Categorize and filter searching tasks according to a product and version.
  • Resources: Track the resources, participants, owner and requestor of a task.
  • Workload Management: Compare the workload of your workers to redistribute tasks optimally.
  • Subtasks: Delegate parts of a task to others, as well as designate and track dependencies.
  • Search Form: Search for tasks by arbitrary information and display the results in related groups for a quick snapshot.
  • Browse Panel: Quickly see what tasks you or your workers have in their queue, with a quick overview of their status.
  • Bulk Changes: Organize and change the specifications, timeline and status of multiple tasks at a time, so as to handle generalized reassignment, project delays, and project redesign.
  • Notification: Keep the task owner and task requestor notified via email when a task changes.
  • Notes: Keep track of all important changes to a task throughout it's lifetime, as well as keep arbitrary notes for future reference.
  • Gantt Charts: View a graphical timeline of a task and it's subtasks/dependencies.
  • Task Specialization: Categorize tasks based upon their purpose, and associate workers with specific categories, making assignment to qualified workers simple.
Overview:

Here's what the module's major parts look like:

Task Management Console:
The Task Management Console contains the main page to access your or another's current task queue and to search for tasks by information it has.
Task Browse Page:
The Task Browse Page lets you see what tasks you have been assigned to finish, or has been assigned to any or all of the people you manage.
Task Search Page:
The Task Search Page is where you can find tasks (whether completed or not) that belong to you or others, based upon any information.
Task Edit Page:
The Task Edit Page is where all changes to tasks occur, as well as where changes are viewed by others.
Getting There:
Task Management Console:
From the main window, click on the 'Tasks' tab in the upper right corner of the screen to get to the general Task Management Console.
Task Search Page:
Once inside the Task Management Console (after clicking on the 'Tasks' tab), clicking on the 'Search' navigation link (in the upper right, below the tabs) to get to the Task Search page.
Task Browse Page:
From the Task Management Console, you can click on the 'Browse' navigation link to get to a list of pending tasks that you (or another person that you manage) have.
Task Add Page:
From the Task Management Console, you can add a new task by clicking on the 'Add' navigation link. A separate window will open for this purpose. After clicking on the 'Save' button in the window, it will go to the Task Edit page for the respective task.
Task Edit Page:
From either the Task Search or Task Browse page, you can click on an individual task's ID or Title to open the window for changing the task (it's requirements, status, etc.).
