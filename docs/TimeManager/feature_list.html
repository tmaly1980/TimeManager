Product Feature List
  • Task Management
    • Timeline: Keep track of when and in how much time you want a task done, as well as get estimates from the task's owner.
    • Categorization: Organize tasks by it's purpose, and automatically assign it to someone qualified.
    • Products: Categorize and filter searching tasks according to a product and version.
    • Search Form: Search for tasks by arbitrary information and display the results in related groups for a quick snapshot.
    • Workload Management: Compare the workload of your workers to redistribute tasks optimally.
    • Resources: Track the resources, participants, owner and requestor of a task.
    • Progress: Manage and monitor the progress of a task, including it's status, cost and what stage it's in (relative to what kind of task it is). Store information in an arbitrary amount of notes.
    • Task Specifications: Give a task a brief summary, an extensive description, priority and what product (if any) it belongs to.
    • Subtasks: Delegate parts of a task to others, as well as designate and track dependencies.
    • Bulk Changes: Organize and change the specifications, timeline and status of multiple tasks at a time, so as to handle generalized reassignment, project delays, and project redesign.
    • Browse Panel: Quickly see what tasks you or your workers have in their queue, with a quick overview of their status.
    • Notification: Keep the task owner and task requestor notified via email when a task changes.
    • Notes: Keep track of all important changes to a task throughout it's lifetime, as well as keep arbitrary notes for future reference.
    • Gantt Charts: View a graphical timeline of a task and it's subtasks/dependencies.
    • Task Specialization: Categorize tasks based upon their purpose, and associate workers with specific categories, making assignment to qualified workers simple.
    • Show/Hide (Power/Basic User modes): Showing or hiding sections of a task for the complexity or simplicity that you want.
  • Project Management
    • Project Searching: Find the projects you want to work and check up on.
    • Participant List: Get others to help you complete a project, by working on the tasks
    • Project Specification: Define what a project is and what you expect out of it, including dates, budget, priority and resources.
    • Browse Panel: Quickly see what you or your managed workers are working on, with a brief status.
    • Project Overview Panel: View a quick snapshot of the overall specifications and status of a project, including the status of the milestones.
    • Milestones / Task Groups: Designate goals and major markers within a project, and subdivide each of them into groups of tasks.
    • Gantt Chart: View a graphical timeline with status of the tasks in a project.
  • Administration
    • User Account Management: View and manage user accounts, their task category assignments, as well as browse a person's workload
    • Workload Comparison: View and compare the workload of one or more people, and redistribute work if desired
    • Group Account Management: Create and modify groups, designate the manager, create task categories with stages, and grant users membership and management from the group's manager
    • Product Management: Designate the products within the company, along with revisions/versions for each
  • Security
    • Task Requestor Access: Permit the requestor of a task (and no one else) to create, reassign and alter the specifications of a task
    • Task Owner Access: Permit the task owner to alter the status of a task, without permitting changes to the task's requirements and specifications
    • Non-Requestor Managerial Access: Permit managers of a task owner, other than the task's requestor, to view, but not alter their tasks
    • Other Access: All other people will not be able to find, look at or alter tasks and projects of others that they have no managerial access over
  • Help System
  • Dashboard
    • Common Actions: Jump to do common actions such as adding and searching tasks and projects, as well as browsing the documentation
    • My Tasks/Projects: Show the tasks and projects that you have due this and next week
    • My Workers' Tasks/Projects: Keep track of tasks and projects that your workers have due this and next week
