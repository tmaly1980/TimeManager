Time Manager: Task Management: Workload Management
Description:

The Workload Management Console provides a means to monitor how much work (displayed as hours allocated versus hours available) that one or more workers have over a period of any number of weeks. You can compare several workers prior to task assignment so as to assign the task to the best person. The console also provides a means to redistribute tasks that you want to give to someone else.

Items:

This feature consists of the following items (marked on the overview images by the respective number):

  1. Workload Comparison: Compare the workload among arbitrary team members, from within the Administration Console.
  2. Workload Trends: See how one or more person's workload changes over time, to know when to best assign and redistribute tasks.
  3. Workload Redistribution: After comparing the workload of multiple people over multiple weeks, quickly redistribute tasks for maximum efficiency.
