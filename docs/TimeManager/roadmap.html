Product Road Map (Sneak Peak)

Below is our joint vision required to make Time Manager a competetive and robust product to dynamically suit your needs.

Time Manager 2.0 Enhancements (This Version) :

Product Goal:

  • Organizing tasks into groups (Task Groups), independent of projects
  • Improved user interface so as to maximize productivity
  • Organizing tasks and projects into products (and specific versions)

Product Component:

  • Gantt Timeline Charts for Milestones and Parent Tasks
  • Dashboard with links to common things to do, and snapshot of tasks/projects due soon

Component Goal:

  • Allowing easy changes to multiple tasks, such as for readjusting dates due to delays

Component Feature:

  • Implementing subtasks and dependencies to permit easy oversight
  • Documenting the stage of a task, based upon a pre-defined list of stages for the task's category
  • Supplementary list of participants within a task
  • Listing the resources and web pages involved with a task

Time Manager 3.0 Enhancements (Q4 2004) :

Product Goal:

  • Accurately predicting timelines for product releases, as well as understanding delays

Product Component:

  • Software Project Management using a predefined workflow
  • Product Management Console

Component Goal:

  • Software Documentation and Marketing Management
  • Managing product goals, associating/satisfying with tasks and projects
  • Tracking and rating product competitors, with extensive ratings and tracking features to duplicate

Component Feature:

  • Feature Requests
  • Product Architecture Management
