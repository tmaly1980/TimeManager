Product Tutorial: Using the Calendar

The calendar provides a way to set dates visually, such as when you know the day of the week, etc... without knowing (nor wanting to know) the exact numerical date.

Sections:

  1. Opening the Calendar window
  2. Navigating the month
  3. Navigation the year
  4. Navigating to this month
  5. Setting the date
  1. Opening the Calendar window:

    2. Next to any date field, there should be a button that says 'Calendar' or 'Set'. Clicking on it opens a window that will put a date into that field.

  2. Navigating the month:

    3. By clicking on the single arrows, you can move back and forth through the months.

  3. Navigation the year:

    4. By clicking on the double arrows, you can move back and forth a year at a time.

  4. Navigating to this month:

    5. By clicking on the month/year date (in between the navigational arrows), one can quickly go back to this day's month. Today will show up in a different color than every other day.

  5. Setting the date:

    6. By clicking on a day in the calendar, you will then have the date field in the other window be set. To close the calendar window, click on 'Close'.
